## Notes

---

This raid build focuses on utilizing Warrior's strong Dual Wielding trait on Paragon, allowing for a more selfless low intensity DPS that's great for group content. 

Consider it a more selfless alternative to the [Magebane Daggerbreaker](http://https://aw2.help/builds/power-spellbreaker/) build.

## Notes
--- 

### Relic
Upkeep Relic of the Claw with {{ skill(id="14415") }} (Mace 5) and {{ skill(id="44937") }} (Dagger 3). The rotation is made in such a way that you are reaching near 100% uptime of Claw without much thought.

You can also use Relic of the Eagle for a DPS loss of ~3k.

### Chants

Your F2 is what you will be camping most of the time. However, don't forget your other chants.

Most notably, {{ skill(id="77155") }} is a group stun break and also gives stability. To use it, simply press it, then immediately use your F2 again; it's a major DPS loss to forget to head back to your F2 chant, so make sure to do so.

### Utilities and Elites

{{ skill(id="14410") }} is the only utility skill required for this build, as removing all of the other Signets results in only a 3k DPS loss. When the build already is getting 36k, this is more than acceptable if you're willing to bring utility for your team. {{ skill(id="14404") }} is the last Signet you'd want to swap out as it's a pure DPS increase, but it is still worth swapping off if your team needs more utility.

Consider:

{{ skill(id="14354") }} for long-range immobilization.

{{ skill(id="14502") }}, {{ skill(id="14516") }}, {{ skill(id="77114") }} and {{ skill(id="14405") }} for more CC.

{{ skill(id="14408") }} for superspeed, stability, resistance and group stun break.

{{ skill(id="76769") }} for resistance, aegis, resolution, and group stun break.

{{ skill(id="14528") }} for barrier or aegis.

{{ skill(id="76934") }} for barrier.

{{ skill(id="14392") }}, {{ skill(id="21815") }} or {{ skill(id="76562") for ignoring damage, allowing you (or your group with We Will Never Yield) to skip mechanics like Deimos's Mind Crush.
