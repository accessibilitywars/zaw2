- Armor: Bringer's Leggings, the rest as Sinister pieces, with Lyssa Runes
- Trinkets:

  - Rampager's Rings and Backpiece
  - Bringer's Accessory
- Food & Utility: Koi Cake, Toxic Focusing Crystal
